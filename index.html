Nope is a film I saw later in its release window due to hearing of audience disappointment on the net. I hear their criticisms but I am honestly so glad I did not miss out on this one. Jordan Peele in an interview stated he was out to make a spectacle and he delivered on that front. The film also has some to say about spectacle. Without spoiling too much, Nope is a blast of a cinematic experience with top-tier camera-work and creative visuals, and let's not forget the great characters either. Is there a lot of unanswered questions? Sure. Is there a lot that doesn't make a ton of sense? Sure, so focus on the well-done aspects of the film and you'll come out happy.

Everything Everywhere All at Once... What a movie, movie of the year. The less you know about it before going into it the better. The way in which its concept is executed is highly refreshing. I laughed in this film more than I have in maybe any other. Some very off-the-wall sequences. A lot of references. But most of all, a beautiful heart. Think, the creativity and the immersiveness of the Matrix, and the heart of Encanto.

Encanto! I work with children. I heard them singing the songs of Encanto before I came around to watching it. I am not a fan of Frozen, not very much a Disney fan. But I love how this film focuses on family dynamic. Some might be disappointed that its scale is small, but it makes it a more intimate piece. Great story, great visuals, the voice actress of Mirabel is fantastic. The whole cast is fantastic. I say Moana is the navigator... steering Disney in a good direction. Encanto and before, Coco, have taken Moana but with more focus on the family with a better set of conflicts, unless you like action.

Primal is not a movie... Actually, nevermind! It is now, its really just its first four episodes strung together. One word: phenomenal. An absolutely brutal world, follows travails of human and dinosaur. How was their bond forged, how does it remain? So, so touching, and truly made me a little more grateful for what I have in my life. Strong is the spirit that has weathered many tragedies.
